The "Phyllocephalus" group of Haplopappus currently is under study by
Hartman and Lane (Hartman k Lane 1984; Lane 1982; Lane k Hartman 1984;
Lane k Hartman 1985; Lane, et al. 1987). It is part of an i = 6 chromosomal
line which includes Isocoma, Stephanodona, Grindelia, Xanthocephalum, Oli-
vaea, Pnonopsis, probably Pyrrocoma and Oonopsis, and perhaps Xylorhiza.
