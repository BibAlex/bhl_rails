NOTE ADDED IN PROOF
The present composition of the tribe Gilieae as oudined above needs to be
qualified. The five genera listed are the extant members of the tribe. Lott et al. (1998)
have recendy discovered and described a mid-Eocene genus, Gilisenium, which
belongs in the Gilieae and is close to Gilia. This important discovery shows that the
tribe is much older than we and others have thought.
