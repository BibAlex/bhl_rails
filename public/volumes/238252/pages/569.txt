THE PEOGEESS OF SCIENCE.
THE CENTENARY OF THE DEATH
OF PRIESTLEY.
Priestley died on February 6, 1804,
and the centenary of his death has been
commemorated in Great Britain and
in the United States. Thirty years
ago the centenary of the discovery of
oxygen was celebrated, and at that time
this magazine published several arti-
cles on the life and work of Priestley.
In the issue for August, 1874, a bio-
graphical sketch and an appreciation by
Dr. John W. Draper will be found. An
address given by Huxley on the occasion
Joseph Priestley.
