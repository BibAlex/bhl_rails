ROYAL SOCIETY OF CANADA.
PKOOEEDINGS FOE 1901.
TWENTIETH GENERAL MEETING.
SESSION I. (xMay 21st.)
The Eoyal Society of Canada held its twentieth general meeting
in the Assembly Hall of the Normal School at Ottawa, on Tuesday,
May 21st. The President, Dr. Louis Frechette, C.M.G., took the chair
at 10 o'clock a.m., and formally called the meeting to order.
The Honorary Secretary, Sir John Bourinot, K.C.M.G., read the
list of members, and the following gentlemen answered to their names:
