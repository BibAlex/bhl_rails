Karny, in his revision, overlooked Dohrn 's Indo-Malayan Cono-
cephalid paper of 1905, in which three species of this genus were
described, and as a result described the female of the present
species as bimaculata, belonging to a series in which "dorso medio
pronoti toto nigro differunt." This difference in pronotal marking
constitutes one of the most striking color features to separate
thoracica from bimaculata.
