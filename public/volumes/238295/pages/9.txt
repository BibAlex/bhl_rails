ABSTRACT
A new species, Crossothamnus pascoanus, is described from
Pasco, Peru. It is closely related to C. weberbauen (Hieron.) King & H.
Robins, in gynoecied characters, possessing broad smooth style branches
and a glabrous node at the base of the shaft.
KEY WORDS: Crossothamnus, Asteraceae, Eupatorieae, Peru
Routine identification of Peruvian Asteraceae has revealed the following
novelty.

Shrub to 1 m high. Stems densely purplish pilose. Leaves opposite, thick
