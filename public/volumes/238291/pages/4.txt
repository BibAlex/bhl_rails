Phytologia (June 1997) 82(6);385-387.
NEW COMBINATIONS IN MEXICAN SPECIES OF EUPATORIEAE
(ASTERACEAE)
B. L. Turner
Department of Botany, University of Texas, Austin, Texas 78713 U.S.A.
ABSTRACT
In order to accommodate a revised floristic study of the tribe Eupatorieae
for Mexico, the following new combinations are proposed: Bartlettina
calderonii (B.L. Turner) B.L. Turner {=Eupatorium calderonii B.L. Turner);
B. lanicaulis (B.L. Rob.) B.L. Turner {=Eupatorium lanicaule B.L. Rob.);
B. xalapana (B.L. Turner) B.L. Turner {=Eupatorium xalapanum B.L.
Tumer); Chromolaena guiengolensis (L. Torres & Villaseiior) B.L.
Tumer {=Eupatorium guiengolense L. Torres & Villaseiior); Chromolaena
tamaulipasensis (King & H. Rob.) B.L. Tumer {=Viereckia tamaulipasense
King & H. Rob.); Critonia microdon (B.L. Rob.) B.L. Tumer
{=Eupatorium microdon B.L. Rob.); Koanophyllon galeana (B.L. Tumer)
B.L. Tumer {=Eupatorium galeanum B.L. Tumer); Koanophyllon
peninsularis (Brandegee) B.L. Tumer {=Eupatorium peninsulare
Brandegee); Kyrsteniopsis eriocarpa (B.L. Rob. & Greenm.) B.L.
Tumer (=Eupatonum eriocarpum B.L. Rob. & Greenm.); Kyrsteniopsis
heathiae (B.L. Tumer) B.L. Tumer (=Eupatorium heathiae B.L. Tumer);
Kyrsteniopsis iltisii (King & H. Rob.) B.L. Tumer (=Critonia iltisii King
& H. Rob.; Kyrsteniopsis perpetiolata King & H. Rob.
{=Pseudokyrsteniopsis perpetiolata King & H. Rob.); Kyrsteniopsis
spinaciifolia (DC.) B.L. Tumer {=Bulbostylis spinaciifolia DC).
KEY WORDS: Asteraceae, Eupatorieae, Mexico, Bartlettina, Chromolaena,
Critonia, Eupatorium, Koanophyllon, Kyrsteniopsis, nomenclature
In connection with a reedited revisionary study of the tribe Eupatorieae for Mexico
(Tumer 1997), and to accommodate an ongoing inventory of the comps of Mexico, the
following new combinations are proposed.
