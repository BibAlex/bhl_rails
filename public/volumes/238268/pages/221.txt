THIS species inhabits Cayenne : it is in length
thirteen inches : its beak is brown above and
yellowish beneath : the prevailing colour of its
plumage is grey : the wings are nearly the length
of the tail, are dusky black in colour, with pale
ash-coloured bands : tail brownish grey, barred
with brown.
