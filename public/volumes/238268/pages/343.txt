THE Frivolous Thrush inhabits the neighbourhood
of Port Jackson : its beak is black : the upper parts
of its plumage are brown : the forehead and front
of the crown of the head varied with ash-colour
and white : quills light brown : under parts of the
body white, inclining to rufous on the sides of the
neck and breast, and to yellow under the wings :
tail dark brown, and short : legs lead-colour.
