INHABITS Cayenne : length four inches : beak
dusky: throat and chin black: breast and upper
part of the belly white : breast and sides spotted
with black : upper parts of the body, quills, and
tail brown : wings with two white bars : lower
part of the back, some of the secondaries on their
outer web, and tip of the tail, white : lower belly,
thighs, and vent, orange : legs brown.
