Aoonalaschkan Swallow. Lath. Gen. Syn. 4. 571. 15.
THIS, as its name implies, inhabits Aoonalaschka :
it is in length four inches and a half: its beak is
dusky : the prevailing colour of the plumage is
obscure black ; with the sides of the head, and
under parts of the body, dusky cinereous : rump
dirty white : tail slightly forked : legs dusky.
