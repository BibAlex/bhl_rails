EUROPEAN WATEROUZEL. Si?
swim on the surface of the water, but will often
float with the stream and dive down for its prey :
it has been taken with a line and hook which were
baited to catch fish.
Inhabits the whole of Europe, Italy excepted :
its song is very harmonious, and is begun very
early in the season, even during a hard frost : its
flight is similar to that of the Kingsfisher.
