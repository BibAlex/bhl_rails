BEAK black : forehead with a rusty yellow spot :
all the upper parts of the body and collar round
the neck of a silky black : lesser wing-coverts
with a violet gloss : throat rusty yellow : under parts
of the body white : tail-feathers of a shining black
colour : legs black. Inhabits the isle of Panay.
