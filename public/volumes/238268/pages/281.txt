Yellow-bellied Thrush. Lath. Gen. Syn. 3. 42. 45.
THE Yellow-bellied Thrush is a native of Brasil :
it is the size of a Redwing : its beak and upper
part of the plumage are black : the middle of the
wing with a white stripe : lower part of the back
and rump ferruginous : under parts of the body
dirty yellow, palest on the chin and fore-part of
the neck : sides of the body transversely striped
with dusky : tail somewhat wedged ; the outer
feather white, the two next tipped, and the rest
merely fringed with that colour : legs brown.
