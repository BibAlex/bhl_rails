26 WHITE-THROATED MAN AKIN.
WHITE-FRONTED MANAKIN.
(Pipra serena.)
I
Pi. nigra,fronte alba, uropygio cyaneo, venire julvo.
Black Manakin, with the forehead white, rump blue, and belly
fulvous.
Pipra serena. Lin. Syst. Nat. 1. 340. 11. Gmel. Syst. Nat. l.
1002. Lath. Ind. Orn. 2. 556. 5.
Manacus alba fronte. Briss. 4. 457- Q. pi. 36. f. 2.
Le Manakin vane". Buff: Hist. Nat. Ois. 4. 423. t. \Q\.f. 1.
Manakin a front blanc de Cayenne. Buff: PI. Enl. 324. f. 1.
White fronted Manakin. Lath. Gen. Syn. 4. 621. 3.
THIS inhabits the same countries as the gene-
rality of the species of the genus : it is the size of
a Wren : length three and a half inches : beak
black: feathers on the forehead black at the base,
blue in the middle, and white at the tips : those
of the crown without the white ; which makes it
appear blue: belly, thighs, and vent, orange: rump
blue : rest of the plumage of a shining blue black :
legs black.
