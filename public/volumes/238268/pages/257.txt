THE Sooty Thrush is the size of the common
Thrush : its beak is pale : the prevailing colour
of its plumage is dark greenish brown : the chin
and fore-part of the neck pale grey : the breast rs
sprinkled with large dusky spots : the legs are
yellowish. Inhabits New Holland.
