LENGTH twelve and a half inches : beak grey,
with a black tip : head, neck, and breast, tinged
with purplish ; the feathers on- the top, and hind
parts of the former, green, and formed into a crest:
rest of the upper parts pale grey: quills and tail
grey brown, the latter inclining to blue : middle
tail-feathers above eight inches in length ; outer
ones scarcely an inch. Native of Senegal.
